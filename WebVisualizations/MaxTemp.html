Max Temperature


As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg Latitude). More interstingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northen hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of year this data was taken.
